About the Designer

Joanna is a visionary interior designer renowned for her distinctive style and unrivaled ability to curate captivating living spaces. With over fifteen years of experience, Joanna has honed her craft, elevating spaces of all types and sizes, from contemporary apartments to traditional homes, singular spaces to entire renovations.

Joanna's mastery lies in her ability to create harmonious compositions, effortlessly blending modern elements with timeless aesthetics, while striking a delicate balance between form and function. Her background in visual art, print-making, and jewelry design infuses her work with distinct artistic detail, making her uniquely suited as a designer. Every detail serves a purpose, leaving spaces that are both intriguing and inviting.
